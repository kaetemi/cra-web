What to look for:
  • Browser scaling (left) darkens midtones and can cause color shifts, especially visible in gradients and thin lines
  • Linear RGB scaling (right) preserves brightness and color accuracy
  • The difference is most visible when downscaling by 2x or more
  • Lanczos also produces sharper results than bilinear/bicubic
